The vision of learning precision health is to leverage technology, data analytics, and evidence-based practices to create a feedback loop that continuously informs and improves healthcare delivery, policy, and practice. The goal is to prevent disease occurrence, detect diseases earlier, and advance human health.

This workshop will provide an overview of learning precision health, highlighting the role of real-world evidence in improving patient outcomes. Participants will gain insights into the foundational principles of learning precision health, the technologies that support them, and practical steps for their implementation in cancer, aging, psychiatry, and rare diseases.
